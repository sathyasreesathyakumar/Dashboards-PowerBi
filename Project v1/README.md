# ☕ Coffee Business Intelligence Centre Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Coffee Business Intelligence Centre Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze coffee sales performance, customer purchasing trends, product contributions, and business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-performing products, understand revenue distribution, and make data-driven decisions.

This project demonstrates advanced Power BI dashboard development using DAX measures, Pareto Analysis, KPI reporting, scatter plot analysis, and business storytelling techniques.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Track total revenue and order volume.
* Identify top-performing coffee products.
* Analyze peak sales hours.
* Understand product contribution to revenue.
* Discover relationships between orders, revenue, and product performance.
* Generate actionable business insights.

## 📊 Dashboard Features
### KPI Cards
The dashboard includes key performance indicators:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Top Selling Product
* Peak Sales Hour

### Revenue Trend Analysis
Tracks revenue performance over time to identify growth patterns, seasonal trends, and sales fluctuations.

**Visualization Used:** Line Chart

### Revenue Share by Coffee Type

Shows the contribution of each coffee category toward total revenue.

**Visualization Used:** Donut Chart

### Weekday × Hour Sales Analysis

Analyzes customer purchasing behavior across weekdays and business hours.

**Visualization Used:** Matrix Table

### Pareto Analysis

Identifies products contributing the highest percentage of revenue using the 80/20 principle.

**Visualization Used:**

* Column Chart
* Cumulative Revenue Percentage Line

### Product Performance Matrix

Examines relationships between:

* Total Orders
* Total Revenue
* Average Revenue

to identify high-performing and low-performing coffee products.

**Visualization Used:** Scatter Plot

### Smart Insights Panel

Provides automated business insights using DAX calculations.

Example insights include:

* Highest revenue-generating product
* Lowest-performing product
* Revenue contribution percentages
* Product performance comparisons

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* Latte generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Peak sales occurred at 10 AM.
* Revenue is concentrated among a small number of coffee products.
* Product performance varies significantly across categories.
* Customer purchase behavior differs by weekday and hour.
